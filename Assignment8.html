3. Description List - Animal Types & Description

Mammals
Warm-blooded animals that usually have fur or hair and give birth to live young.
Birds
Feathered, winged animals that lay eggs and most can fly.
Reptiles
Cold-blooded vertebrates like snakes, lizards, and turtles with scaly skin.
Amphibians
Animals that live both in water and on land, such as frogs and salamanders.
Insects
Small six-legged creatures like ants, butterflies, and bees.
